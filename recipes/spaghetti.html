Lasagna

Description

This is a delicious lasagna recipe that's perfect for family gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (16 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, cook and stir ground beef until browned.
  2. Add chopped onions and garlic; cook until onions are transparent.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes and tomato paste; season with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.
